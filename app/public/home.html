Gun Violence Map


Welcome to our site. Our goal is to inform our visitors with data regarding gun violence in the United States.
We wish that our data visualization on this information via chloropeth map on the next page can help keep more people safe.


Use the data however you wish, but keep in mind we've chose this as our project as a mission to show
everyone that gun violence is a real issue here in America and some areas are more concentrated than others.
So either use our map to stay clear of those areas... or to help reform those areas, that's entirely on you.
